    About

    I was born in Whittier, California on March 16, 1995. My parents raised me in Diamond Bar, California and blessed me with three beautiful, younger siblings: Brandon, Nastassia, and Monique. Being the oldest, I strive for the greatest and do everything in my power to set a good example for my younger siblings.

    I graduated from Cal Poly Pomona on June 10th, 2018 with my B.S. in Computer Engineering.

    I love surfing, photography, and most of all, music. Music is an important part of my life. In my free time you can catch me playing guitar, ukulele, piano, drums, and a variety of other instruments. I also enjoy dancing and learning about the Polynesian culture. I’m lucky enough to get to perform and share my talents on the stage with the Ohana Polynesian Dancers.
